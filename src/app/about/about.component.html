Nike

Founder(s): Bill Bowerman, Phil Knight

Nike's journey began with a handshake between two visionaries, Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, who shared a passion for running and a desire to revolutionize athletic gear. From humble beginnings selling shoes out of the trunk of a car, Nike grew into a cultural phenomenon, shaping the sports industry with iconic designs and breakthrough technologies.

Our Mission

Nike's mission is simple yet powerful: to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. We believe that if you have a body, you are an athlete, and our goal is to help you unleash your full potential.

Our Vision

At Nike, innovation is at the core of everything we do. We constantly push the boundaries of design and technology to create products that enhance performance and elevate style. From Nike Air cushioning to Flyknit technology, our innovations have redefined the way athletes train, compete, and express themselves.

Commitment to Sustainability

Beyond performance, Nike is committed to sustainability and responsible business practices. We strive to minimize our environmental footprint, promote diversity and inclusion, and empower communities through sport.
